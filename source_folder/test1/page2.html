His Food


His main diet is the hedgehog food, but Siegfried enjoys a variety of snacks.

His favorite, like most of the other hedgehogs, are mealworms. But I can't feed him theese often because they are quite expensive. Most of the time I give him papayas and pineapples. They say hedgehogs like things like cabbages and nuts, but Siegfried doesn't seem to be interested in them.
