His Life

Treadmill

Hedgehogs are crazy fan of treadmills, and Siegfried is not an exception.
You can hear him run all night long.

Sleeping

Siegfried spends most of the time sleeping.
It's said that the lack of need for hunting made domesticated hedgehogs' sleeping hours to increase.

Drinking Water

Domesticated hedgehogs are originally from African deserts.
So they are used to enduring thirst but at the same time love drinking water. After a bit of excercise Siegfried always stops by to get a drink.

Porcupine

When in tension, hedgehogs roll up their body and take a defensive form.
And when it does, it takes a figure of a porcupine or chestnut. It won't let it's stance down until it's sure that it's safe to do so.
